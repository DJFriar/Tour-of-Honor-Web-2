Tour of Honor is a great reason to hit the open road, honor our nation's heroes, and contribute to a few good charities. The event is a season-long, self-directed ride to memorials and monuments around the continental U.S., Alaska, and Hawaii with any route you choose.

2020 is the 10th Anniversary for Tour of Honor and we'll be featuring the "Best of the Best" memorial sites visited over the past decade, plus some outstanding new ones!

Pins and finishers certificates will be given to all who visit a minimum of seven sites. A "Jack Shoalmire Achievement Award" will be awarded to the person visiting the most memorial sites during 2020. Top 10 riders will receive flag #s 1 through 10 in 2021.

NOTE: DUE TO COVID-19 AND CERTAIN STATES LOCKED DOWN WITH INDEFINITE CLOSURES, FOR THE SAFETY AND FAIRNESS TO ALL RIDERS, THERE WILL BE NO TROPHIES EARNED IN 2020. TROPHY COSTS WILL BE DIVIDED EQUALLY BETWEEN THE THREE CHARITIES THAT TOUR OF HONOR RIDERS SUPPORT.

According to rider preference, $25 from each paid registration will go to Fisher House Foundation, Gold Star Family Memorial Foundation, or Police and Fire: The Fallen Heroes. All profits after expenses will be divided equally between the three charities.
